Frequently Asked Questions

Qn 1: What are the common menstrual cycle lengths?

Ans: The average menstrual cycle length ranges from 21 to 35 days, with 28 days being the most common.

Qn 2: How can I track my menstrual cycle?

Ans: You can track your menstrual cycle by noting the start and end dates of your periods and calculating the average cycle length over several months.

Qn 3: Are there any natural remedies for menstrual cramps?

Ans: Yes, some natural remedies for menstrual cramps include applying heat, taking over-the-counter pain relievers, and practicing relaxation techniques.

Qn 4: Can I use CodeRed on my mobile device?

Ans: Yes, CodeRed is designed to be mobile-friendly and can be accessed on various devices, including smartphones and tablets.

Qn 5: Can I customize the period prediction settings?

Ans: Yes, you can customize the period prediction settings based on your individual cycle length and preferences. The prediction algorithm takes your input into account to provide more accurate predictions.
