About CodeRed

CodeRed is a period tracker website that aims to provide women with accurate predictions and helpful information about their menstrual cycles. Our mission is to empower women to better understand their bodies and make informed decisions regarding their menstrual health.

With CodeRed, you can easily track your menstrual cycles, receive predictions for your next period, and gain insights into your menstrual health patterns. Our user-friendly interface and reliable predictions make it easy for you to stay informed and prepared.

Stay connected with us to access the latest updates, tips, and resources related to menstrual health and hygiene. We are committed to providing a safe and inclusive space for all individuals to learn, share, and support each other.
