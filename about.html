About CodeRed

CodeRed is a period tracker website designed to help women track and manage their menstrual cycles with ease and accuracy. We understand the importance of menstrual health and strive to provide women with a reliable tool for tracking their periods and predicting future cycles.

With CodeRed, you can conveniently input your last period start date and average cycle length to receive predictions for your next period. Our prediction algorithm takes into account various factors to provide you with an estimated date, helping you plan and prepare for your menstrual cycle.

We believe in empowering women by providing them with knowledge and information about their menstrual health. That's why CodeRed not only offers period tracking features but also provides resources and articles on menstrual health and hygiene. Our goal is to educate and support women in understanding their bodies and making informed choices.

CodeRed values user privacy and data security. All personal information provided for tracking purposes is handled with the utmost care and stored securely. We are committed to maintaining a safe and confidential environment for our users.
